What is PixelRealEstate?

PixelRealEstate is a constantly evolving canvas of a tradable digital property where the owners of property may upload any image they like, or rent their property to other users.

What is Digital Property?

Digital properties in PixelRealEstate are tradable blockchain tokens powered by Ethereum. Each property is a separate individual token, which can have its appearance altered to its owners desire. Where Bitcoin and Ethereum are cryptocurrencies, these are cryptocollectables. Cryptocollectables can be bought, sold, or traded like any collectable item.

Interested in jumping in?

Viewing the digital canvas does not require any extra setup You can checkout the canvas on our Canvas page.

When you want to interact with the canvas throguh our website, you must have an Ethereum account, as well was have metamask installed. You can view the Setting Up page for simple instructions on installing MetaMask.

Getting Started

PixelRealEstate.io uses MetaMask on our website for interacting with the PixelRealEstate Ethereum Smart Contract. What this means is we require users to install a simple tool known as MetaMask to simplify the experience, letting you jump in easier.

What do I need to use PixelRealEstate?

To use PixelRealEstate through [PixelRealEstate.io] you will need

  • A computer running [Brave], [FireFox], or [Chrome]
  • [MetaMask] installed, which allows us to mor easily communicate with the digital real estate market
  • Ether (ETH) • a form of digital currenchy that PixelRealEstate uses in it's digital real estate economy

Installing MetaMask

PixelRealEstate uses MetaMask to communicate with the digital real estate marlet, which is run on a Smart Contract through Ethereum. MetaMask is a digital wallet for Ethereum, meaning your funds ar stored securely in this wallet, rather than you having to trust us with your funds through our website. Remember, MetaMask is a wallet, so protect it. Your MetaMask passwords or seed words must be kept secret, but don't lose them!

We suggest printing a paper copy off of your password and seed words, then putting it somewhere for safe keeping.
These cannot be recovered if you lose them.

How do I get ETH?

For U.S. citizens, ETH can be bought within MetaMask itself.

For non U.S. citizens, you will have to buy it from exchanges. Exchanges that you can buy ETH form include [Coinbase], [Quadriga], and [Kraken] to name a few.

PixelRealEstate is not affiliated with any of the list exchanges

How do I send ETH to MetaMask?

Once you have acquired ETH on the exchanges of your choice, you must it to your MetaMask ETH address.

You can find your MetaMask ETH address by clicking on the '...' menu button, then selecting 'Copy to Clipboard'. At this point, the address is copied, so when you send your ETH out of the exchange, you can simply paste the address in.

For Press

PixelRealEstate are tradable, collectable, digital property. Where each cryptocurrency token is the same as the next (that is, each Bitcoin is is just like any other Bitcoin), each digital property is a unique, non-fungible token. These properties cannot be created, and each represent a different region on the digital real estate market.

Each piece of digital real estate can be modified by its owner to change its appearance, allowing for property owners to customize their desired appearance, as well as change the hover text and clickable link of each property. This means that properties can be used to showcase art, advertise websites, or share images.

Each piece of property can also be rented out, allowing owners who purchase property due to the expectation of equity growth to sit back and build equity while earning payments through renters.

If a user does not feel up for purchasing an entire property, or would like to test the water first before purchasing, they can rent properties from other users on the system. While you rent a property from another user, you are given full control over the property, allowing users to fully maximize their use at a lower cost.

About Us

In PixelRealEstate, property owners own 10x10 regions of pixels which they can customize to their liking visually, set hover text for, clickable links for, rent out to other users, trade and sell.

PixelRealEstate is the world's first digital property market as well as one of the world's first cryptocollectables through blockchain technology. Blockchain technology is the technology that powers Bitcoin, Ethereum and other cryptocollectables. Each piece of property is completely owned by the user, as they are each their own unique Ethereum token.


Technical Details

  1. PixelRealEstate is a Ethereum Smart Contract powered by the Ethereum network.
  2. Each digital property is a unique, non-fungible token on Ethereum, which allows for it’s owner to change it’s display image, hover text, clickable link, as well as can be listed for sale or rent.
  3. There are 10,000 unique properties in the PixelRealEstate digital property market. No new ones will ever be created, nor can any property ever be destroyed.
  4. PixelRealEstate follows a sustainable income model, rather than an ICO. Each property will be available for purchase at launch, however the cost to purchase each property will go up incrementally with each sale.
  5. The starting price for a digital property is 0.01 ETH. Every ten purchases, the price is increased by 0.001 ETH, with that incrementing value going up by 0.0005 ETH every ten purchases as well. This pushes an upwards pressure in the going property evaluation, pushing for sustainable equity growth in the investment of properties.
  6. Properties in PixelRealEstate follow the free market after their initial purchase. Similar to the values of regular properties, stamps or collectable cards, the price of a digital property in the future is determined by pure supply and demand.
